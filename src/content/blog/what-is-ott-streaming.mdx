The way people consume video content has fundamentally changed. Gone are the days of cable TV monopolies — today, **Over-The-Top (OTT)** platforms deliver content directly to viewers over the internet, bypassing traditional distribution channels entirely. But what does it actually take to build and run a modern OTT platform?

## Understanding OTT: Beyond the Basics

OTT streaming refers to any content delivered directly over the internet, without requiring users to subscribe to a traditional cable or satellite provider. Netflix, YouTube, and Disney+ are household names, but the real revolution is happening in **regional and niche OTT platforms**.

### The Three Pillars of OTT

Every successful OTT platform rests on three fundamental pillars:

1. **Content Ingestion & Processing** — Getting raw video from creators into a streamable format
2. **Content Delivery** — Distributing video efficiently to viewers worldwide
3. **Viewer Experience** — The apps, UI, and features that keep audiences engaged

## How Video Streaming Actually Works

When you press "Play" on a video, a remarkable chain of events happens in milliseconds:

```
Raw Video → Transcoding → Packaging → CDN → Adaptive Player → Your Screen
```

### Step 1: Transcoding

Raw video files are enormous — a single hour of 4K footage can be 300+ GB. Transcoding converts this into multiple quality levels:

```javascript
// PlayMist transcoding pipeline configuration
const transcodingProfile = {
  input: 's3://raw-content/movie-2024.mov',
  outputs: [
    { resolution: '3840x2160', bitrate: '15000k', codec: 'h265' },  // 4K
    { resolution: '1920x1080', bitrate: '5000k', codec: 'h264' },   // 1080p
    { resolution: '1280x720', bitrate: '2500k', codec: 'h264' },    // 720p
    { resolution: '854x480', bitrate: '1000k', codec: 'h264' },     // 480p
    { resolution: '640x360', bitrate: '500k', codec: 'h264' },      // 360p
  ],
  packaging: ['HLS', 'DASH'],
  drm: ['widevine', 'fairplay'],
}
```

### Step 2: Adaptive Bitrate Streaming

This is the magic that makes modern streaming smooth. Instead of sending one fixed quality, the player continuously monitors your connection and switches between quality levels seamlessly.

| Connection Speed | Selected Quality | Experience |
|-----------------|-----------------|------------|
| 20+ Mbps | 4K Ultra HD | Crystal clear |
| 8-20 Mbps | 1080p Full HD | Excellent |
| 3-8 Mbps | 720p HD | Good |
| 1-3 Mbps | 480p SD | Acceptable |
| < 1 Mbps | 360p | Watchable |
