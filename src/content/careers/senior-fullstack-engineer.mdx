## About the Role

We're looking for a **Senior Full-Stack Engineer** who can own delivery end-to-end. You'll work across our Go backend and React/Next.js frontend, transforming product goals into a plan, aligning the team, and shipping services reliably. This is a high-impact role where you'll shape technical direction and mentor engineers.

## What You'll Do

- **Design & build Go microservices** — architect scalable backend services that power our SaaS products
- **Build React/Next.js frontend experiences** — create responsive, performant UIs with TypeScript
- **Define APIs** — design and implement REST and gRPC interfaces between services
- **Create technical plans before coding** — write PRDs, technical specs, and task breakdowns with estimates
- **Collaborate across teams** — work with product, design, and QA to deliver features end-to-end
- **Debug complex production issues** — trace problems across distributed systems and resolve them efficiently
- **Mentor team members** — conduct code reviews, share knowledge, and elevate team engineering standards
- **Track milestones and deadlines** — own release plans with rollout strategies and ensure on-time delivery

## What You're Expected to Deliver

- PRD and technical specifications for features you own
- Task breakdowns with accurate time estimates
- Definition of Ready / Done agreements for your team
- Release plans with staged rollout strategies
- Observability implementation — metrics, logs, traces, and SLOs for every service

## Must-Have Requirements

- **5+ years** of experience building SaaS products in production
- Strong proficiency in **Go** — microservices architecture, REST APIs, and concurrency patterns
- Expert-level **React, Next.js, and TypeScript** — component architecture, state management, SSR/SSG
- Deep knowledge of **PostgreSQL** — query optimization, indexing, replication, and clustering
- Experience with **gRPC**, protocol buffers, and service-to-service communication
- Hands-on experience with **Docker and Kubernetes** — container orchestration, deployments, and scaling
- Understanding of **DNS, load balancing**, and CDN fundamentals
- Proficiency with **Git workflows and CI/CD pipelines** — GitHub Actions, automated testing, and deployments
- Proven ability to **lead a small team** — task delegation, sprint planning, and mentorship
- Experience with **performance tuning** — profiling, caching strategies, and database optimization
- Strong **system design** skills — distributed systems, event-driven architecture, and scalability patterns

## Nice-to-Have

- Familiarity with observability tools (Grafana, Prometheus, Jaeger)
- Contributions to open-source projects
- Experience with video streaming or EdTech platforms
