## About the Role

We're looking for a **DevOps Engineer** who is passionate about building reliable, automated infrastructure. You'll be responsible for our cloud infrastructure, CI/CD pipelines, and ensuring our SaaS products maintain high availability. You'll work closely with the engineering team to streamline deployments and establish best practices for infrastructure as code.

## What You'll Do

- **Design and manage CI/CD pipelines** — automate build, test, and deployment workflows using GitHub Actions, ArgoCD, or similar tools
- **Manage Kubernetes clusters** — deploy, scale, and monitor containerized applications across multiple environments
- **Infrastructure as Code** — define and maintain infrastructure using Terraform, Pulumi, or CloudFormation
- **Monitor and optimize** — set up observability stacks (Prometheus, Grafana, Loki) and respond to alerts
- **Ensure security** — implement secrets management, network policies, and vulnerability scanning
- **Optimize costs** — analyze cloud spending and implement resource optimization strategies
- **Automate everything** — eliminate manual processes through scripting and tooling
- **Incident response** — participate in on-call rotations and lead post-incident reviews

## What You're Expected to Deliver

- Zero-downtime deployment pipelines for all production services
- Infrastructure as Code for all cloud resources — no manual provisioning
- Comprehensive monitoring dashboards with actionable alerts
- Disaster recovery plans with tested backup and restore procedures
- Documentation for all infrastructure components and runbooks

## Must-Have Requirements

- **3+ years** of experience in a DevOps or SRE role
- Strong experience with **Linux administration** — systemd, networking, storage, and performance tuning
- Proficiency with **Docker and Kubernetes** — pod management, Helm charts, and cluster operations
- Experience with **cloud platforms** — AWS, GCP, or DigitalOcean
- Hands-on experience with **CI/CD tools** — GitHub Actions, GitLab CI, Jenkins, or ArgoCD
- Knowledge of **Infrastructure as Code** — Terraform, Pulumi, or Ansible
- Experience with **monitoring and observability** — Prometheus, Grafana, ELK stack, or Datadog
- Strong **scripting skills** — Bash, Python, or Go for automation
- Understanding of **networking** — TCP/IP, DNS, load balancers, firewalls, and VPNs
- Familiarity with **secrets management** — Vault, SOPS, or cloud-native solutions

## Nice-to-Have

- Experience with service mesh (Istio, Linkerd)
- Knowledge of video streaming infrastructure (CDN, transcoding pipelines)
- Familiarity with GitOps workflows
- Experience with database administration (PostgreSQL, Redis)
- Certifications in AWS, GCP, or Kubernetes (CKA/CKAD)
