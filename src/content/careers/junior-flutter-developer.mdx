## About Softimist Limited

Softimist Limited is an EdTech and Video Platform Solution provider based in Bangladesh. We build scalable solutions including Learning Management Systems (LMS), Video on Demand (VOD) platforms, serving clients in Bangladesh and internationally.

## About the Role

We're looking for a passionate **Junior Flutter Developer** to join our team and work on modern applications focused on video streaming and EdTech platforms. If you love building smooth user experiences and are eager to learn cutting-edge technologies, this role is for you.

- **Develop and maintain applications** using Flutter
- **Work on video streaming features** and LMS-based applications

- **Write clean, maintainable, and scalable code** following best practices
- **Debug and fix issues** across different devices and platforms

- Strong **problem-solving mindset** with the ability to learn fast and work with modern tools (including AI-assisted development)
- Hands-on experience building and maintaining **production-grade Flutter applications**

- Strong focus on **code quality, scalability, and maintainability**
